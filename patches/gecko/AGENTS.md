# Gecko Patch Workspace (tombstone)

Gecko is **not** a shipped or active Astrohacker Terminal engine (Issue
26072121272459). Chromium is the supported product browser engine.

Do **not** re-open a product pin, rebuild a live fork for shipping, or revive
`ah-geckod` packaging without a **new** issue.

## What remains

- **Historical** patch archives under `patches/gecko/patches/` — immutable
  records of past experimental series. Do not mass-delete `issue-*` folders.
- Hub contract and release authority still apply if a future issue deliberately
  reintroduces Gecko; until then, treat this tree as archive-only.
